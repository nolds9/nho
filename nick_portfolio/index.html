Connect Four

With this Connect 4 game, a user can play against another local user (or his/herself). The app keeps track of how many games Red and Blue have won. The loser of the current game is the first player of the next game

  • Stack: Mongo, Express, jQuery, and Node
  • Date: June 2015

Movie Me

This app allows users to search the NY Times movie review database for reviews they want to read. They can favorite the ones they want to read and access those favorites at a later time.

It was built as a first time experimentation with the MEAN stack. It is a work in progress.

  • Stack: Mongo, Express, AngularJS, and Node
  • Date: June 2015

Writers Hub

With the shrinking of the print industry, writers are finding it more and more difficult to not only publish their work, but even find readers with which to share it.

We believe there is still an audience hungry for new and inventive fiction and non-fiction that doesn’t necessarily travel through the traditional print routes.

Writer’s Hub satisfies both sides: it provides writers with a venue for sharing their stories with an audience, and it allows readers to review those stories while providing valuable feedback.

  • Stack: Ruby on Rails
  • Date: May 2015
